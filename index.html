Interests

I am a member of Penn's Ping-Pong and Running clubs, so most of my athletic ventures are with one of those two groups. My favorite sports are tennis and soccer , and I try to play as much as my college schedule allows. I am also a fan of wilderness survival. Over the summer I love to practice my bow-drill fire making and shelter building with my younger sister, Siegrid. In college, I stick to more "tame" hobbies like yo-yoing, reading, and meditating. Finally, teaching others is a true passion of mine. Leading survival classes at Camp Fitch, helping students with Scratch.mit through Moelis Access Science, and guiding my old Model UN and debate teams to victory are a some of my happiest experiences in life, and for that I am truly blessed.
